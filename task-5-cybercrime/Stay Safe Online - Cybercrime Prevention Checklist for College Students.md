**Stay Safe Online: Cybercrime Prevention Checklist for College Students**



1\. Financial/UPI Safety: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is only required when you are sending funds.

2\. Financial/UPI Safety: Beware of scanning random QR codes sent by strangers on WhatsApp or Telegram, even if they claim it is for cashback.

3\. Verify the source of any email or message asking for personal information by checking the actual sender address, not just the display name.

4\. Use strong, unique passwords for your university portal, email, and banking apps.

5\. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all critical accounts, especially your primary email and GitHub.

6\. Avoid making sensitive transactions (like paying college fees) while connected to open, public Wi-Fi networks in cafes or airports.

7\. Regularly update your laptop's operating system and antivirus software to patch security vulnerabilities.

8\. Reporting: If you suspect you have been scammed, immediately report it to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the Helpline at 1930.
