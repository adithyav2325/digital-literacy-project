**Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students**



Do's:



1. Do review your privacy settings regularly to control who sees your content.
2. Do use LinkedIn actively to showcase your projects, skills, and academic milestones.
3. Do pause and think about the long-term impact before posting or commenting.
4. Do verify information from reliable sources before sharing or retweeting news.
5. Do maintain a respectful and polite tone in all public discussions and forums.



Don'ts:



1. Don't post emotional or angry rants about your professors, college, or peers.
2. Don't share sensitive personal information like your address, phone number, or financial details.
3. Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying, trolling, or online harassment.
4. Don't use unprofessional "text-speak" or slang when messaging recruiters or faculty on professional platforms.
5. Don't assume that "deleting" a post removes it permanently from the internet; screenshots last forever.
